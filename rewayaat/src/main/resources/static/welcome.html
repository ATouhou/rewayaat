Rewayaat.info

Welcome to the Rewayaat Hadith Database Project. This project has the following aims:

Development of a Comprehensive Hadith Database

To collect all the sayings of the Holy Prophet and his family in a common, searchable repository available to everyone.

Leverage External Translation Efforts

Subject to a review process, users can submit improvements to existing hadith translations or add new ones. Over time, the quality of the Rewayaat repository will improve, yielding significant benefits for everyone (In Progress).

Provide a First-Class Search Experience

Whether you're looking for a little inspiration or doing research for academic purposes, count on Rewayaat to help you quickly and intuitively search thousands of hadith in seconds.

Getting Started

Use the search bar at the top of this page to search for hadith using one of the following methods:

Method 1: Simple Query

Enter relevant search terms, separated by a space. Phrases, or search terms that include a space should be surrounded by quotation marks.

For example, to search for hadith that are from the book Al-Kafi, are about intelligence, and are from the Book of Intellect and Ignorance, one would search:

"Al-Kafi"   intelligence   "The Book of Intellect and Ignorance"

Click here to see this query in action!

Method #2: Elastic Search Query String Query

Rewayaat stores all narrations as documents in an Elastic Search index. If you would like to take advantage of specific search capabilities provided by Elastic Search, you may search using valid Elastic Search v5.3 Query String syntax.

For example, to search for hadith that are from the book Al-Kafi, must include the term momin, must not include the term mushrik, possibly include the term اْلِعْلُم and contain a phrase similar to "The momin, and his signs", one can search:

book:"Al-Kafi"   +Momin   -Mushrik   اْلِعْلُم   "The momin and his signs"~2

Click here to see this query in action!

Archived Texts

Help Grow Rewayaat! We are looking to enhance our collection with books of hadith across all Islamic denominations.

Text Submission Guidelines

Provide our team with an electronic format of the book - acceptable formats include: pdf, docx and .txt. Arabic and English versions of the hadith must be included in the supplied text. Reach out to our team at rewayaat.org@gmail.com to get started.
